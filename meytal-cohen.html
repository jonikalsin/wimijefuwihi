Biography

Meytal Cohen was born in Ramat Gan, Israel on August 9, 1983. YouTube drumming sensation who became the founder of the heavy metal band Meytal. She rose to prominence when she and two friends submitted an electric violin version of “Toxicity” by System of the Down for America’s Got Talent. She and Anna Sentina were both founding members of Meytal. She began playing drums when she was 18 but was required to serve two years in the Israeli Defense Forces before continuing her drumming career. She went on to study drumming at the Los Angeles Music Academy.
