What is Ebanie Bridges' boxing record?

Ebanie Bridges, known as the "Blonde Bomber," is a prominent Australian professional boxer with a remarkable career in the sport.

Her journey in boxing has been marked by significant victories, notable achievements, and a distinctive fighting style that has garnered attention within the boxing community.

Professional Boxing Career

Ebanie Bridges made her professional boxing debut on February 8, 2019, against Mahiecka Pareno, securing a victory through a majority decision.

Since then, she has displayed exceptional prowess in the ring, participating in a total of 11 professional fights, with 9 wins and 2 losses.

Her latest fight, a 10-round unanimous decision defeat to Miyo Yoshida on December 9, 2023, marked a significant moment in her career, demonstrating her resilience and determination in the face of formidable opponents.

Notable Victories

Bridges' boxing record boasts victories over formidable opponents, including Shannon O'Connell, María Cecilia Román, and Crystal Hoy, among others. These victories underscore her capabilities as a skilled and tenacious boxer, showcasing her ability to compete at the highest levels of the sport.

World Title Achievements

Bridges has claimed one world title at bantamweight, representing a significant milestone in her career.

Her first world-title win was the IBF belt at bantamweight, achieved through a hard-fought 10-round unanimous decision victory over María Cecilia Román on March 26, 2022.

Additionally, she has successfully defended her title, further solidifying her position as a formidable force in the bantamweight division.

Fighting Style and Strengths

Bridges' fighting style is characterized by a balanced blend of technical prowess, strategic acumen, and relentless determination.

Her ability to adapt to different opponents, coupled with her agility and precision in the ring, has been instrumental in securing her victories and earning her a reputation as a skilled tactician.

Knockout Record

Bridges has displayed impressive knockout power throughout her career, with a total of 4 knockout wins out of her 11 fights, translating to a knockout ratio of 36.4%. This statistic underscores her proficiency in delivering decisive blows and seizing opportunities to finish fights with impressive displays of striking ability.

Milestones and Controversies

Bridges' journey in boxing has been marked by notable milestones, including her world-title win and successful title defense. Her career has not been devoid of controversies, with her bouts against prominent opponents generating significant attention within the boxing community.

 

Overall, Ebanie Bridges' boxing record stands as a testament to her unwavering dedication, exceptional skill set, and remarkable achievements within the sport.

Her ability to overcome challenges, secure victories against formidable opponents, and claim a world title reflects her status as a prominent figure in the bantamweight division.
