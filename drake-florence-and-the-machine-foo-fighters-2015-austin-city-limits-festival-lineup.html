2015 Austin City Limits Festival Lineup Announced

The lineup for the 2015 Austin City Limits Music Festival has officially been announced, and if you're a fan of any decent music at all, you'll probably be interested in at least one of the 130+ artists set to play.

The festival occurs over the course of two weekends -- kind of like Coachella minus the forced cool factor -- in Austin's Zilker Park. Weekend one takes place from October 2 to October 5, and the second weekend is from October 9 to October 11. While the fact that the festival is spread out over two weekends does mean a wide range of acts will be able to take the stage, quite a few of the artists listed will be playing both weekends.

Drake, Foo Fighters, the Weeknd, Alt-J, Brandon Flowers, ASAP Rocky, Hozier and Disclosure are just a few of the artists set to play both weekends. Florence and the Machine, meanwhile, will only take the stage during weekend two, along with Modest Mouse, Elle King and a handful of others. Alt-rockers the Strokes -- who famously performed their first show in over three years back in May 2014 -- will play during the festival's first weekend only, along with Alabama Shakes, Holychild, Wolf Alice and more.

You can head over to the Austin City Limits Music Festival's official website to check out the full lineup for both weekends. Early Bird three-day passes are already sold out, but you can still get tickets for the festival online.
