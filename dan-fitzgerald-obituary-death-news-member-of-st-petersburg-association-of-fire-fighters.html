Dan Fitzgerald Obituary-Death News; Member Of St. Petersburg Association of Fire Fighters

We wish to convey our deepest condolences to you all as we inform you of the demise of our dearly loved brother, Lieutenant Dan Fitzgerald a retired officer.

In addition to being a respected instructor at the fire academy for a considerable amount of time, Lieutenant Fitzgerald was a well-known and respected person in the firefighting world.

The influence of his work was felt throughout the Tampa Bay area, and it influenced the abilities and values of a great number of firemen who are currently working.

Lt. Fitzgerald, who was popularly referred to as “Big Dan,” was more than just a teacher; he was also a guide, a source of inspiration, and a mentor to those who were under his supervision.

Through each and every life that he touched and each and every lesson that he taught, his unwavering dedication to the fire department was made transparent.

The dedication to excellence that Big Dan displayed left an everlasting impression on the firefighting profession and served as a model for those who aspire to become firemen.

A hole has been left behind by the passing of Lieutenant Dan Fitzgerald, and it will not be easy to fill. His legacy, on the other hand, will continue to be carried on by the abilities and information that he imparted to the numerous people who had the good fortune to learn from him.

The larger-than-life personality of Big Dan, along with his genuine concern for both his colleagues and his students, contributed to the development of a sense of camaraderie that went beyond the confines of the classroom.

While we are saddened by the loss of a real luminary in the firefighting community, we also take this opportunity to honor the long-lasting impact that Lieutenant Dan Fitzgerald made on the lives and careers of those he had the privilege of influencing.

His memory will be engraved indelibly into the collective heart of the firefighting family in Tampa Bay, providing as a constant reminder of the commitment, passion, and camaraderie that characterize our honorable profession.
