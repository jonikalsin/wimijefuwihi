Nick Kroll Wife: A Love Story Of Lily Kwong

Nick Kroll is a well-known actor, comedian, writer, and producer who has been in Big Mouth, Kroll Show, The League, and several other television shows and films. But who is the woman behind this witty man? Meet Lily Kwong, a landscape artist, former model, and social entrepreneur who married Kroll in 2020 and has two children.

How They Met?

Kroll and Kwong met in 2018 through mutual acquaintances and immediately hit it off. They kept their love private for a while, but eventually began to share glimpses of their courtship on social media.

In 2019, Kroll praised Kwong’s skill and beauty while posting a snapshot of her botanical art installation at Art Basel in Miami. Kwong also shared some adorable photos of them traveling together, celebrating holidays, and exploring nature.

Nick Kroll and Lily Kwong Marriage

Kroll and Kwong stunned their fans in November 2020 when they announced they had married in a private ceremony.

Their Children

Kwong disclosed a month before their wedding that she was expecting their first child. She shared a photo of her baby bulge with the statement, “Created a little dumpling with my forever person @nickkroll.”

In January 2021, they gave birth to their son, Leo. Kwong posted a blurry photo of their baby boy and wrote, “Our hearts are full.” In September 2023, they welcomed their second child, a daughter called Gaia. Kwong shared a photo of herself cuddling her infant with the comment, “Our little goddess.”

Nick Kroll and Lily Kwong Career

Kroll and Kwong are both successful and creative in their fields. Kroll is a versatile performer who has created and starred in several comedy shows, such as Kroll Show, Big Mouth, and Oh, Hello. He has also appeared in films like Sausage Party, Loving, and Operation Finale.

He is currently working on the fifth season of Big Mouth, an animated series that he co-created and voices several characters in. Kwong is a landscape artist, who has a passion for reconnecting people to nature.

She graduated from Columbia University with a degree in urban studies and founded Studio Lily Kwong, a design firm that focuses on sustainability and social impact. She has worked with brands like Nike, St-Germain, and Google and has been featured in Forbes, Architectural Digest, and Vogue. She also founded Freedom Gardens, a community-driven platform that promotes edible urban gardens.

Nick Kroll and Lily Kwong love

Kroll and Kwong are a natural match, supporting and inspiring one another. They have a passion for the outdoors, art, and humor, and they frequently brag about each other on social media. They also manage their hectic work and home lives, making time for each other and their children. They are a happy and attractive couple who have discovered their soulmates in each other.
