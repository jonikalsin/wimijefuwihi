Monyetta Shaw Biography

Monyetta Shaw a Hollywood actress started her career as a radio presenter before entering in Hollywood as an actress. She worked as a radio personality, who was introduced by the Program Director Michael Tee of Shreveport's 99.7 KMJJ FM, on the acclaimed Daddy Roe show. She also appeared in several films among which 2008's Soul Men gave her a good response and huge popularity.

Monyetta Shaw is happily married to Heath Carter who is an entrepreneur. Monyetta is a mother of two children Madilyn Grace Smith and Mason Evan Smith from her past partner with Ne-Yo.
