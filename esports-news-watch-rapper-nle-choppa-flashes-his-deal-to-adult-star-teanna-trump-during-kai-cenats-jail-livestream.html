Watch: Rapper NLE Choppa FLASHES his deal to adult star Teanna Trump during Kai Cenats jail

Twitch Superstar Kai Cenat recently unveiled that he would host a seven-day live stream. The entire stream will be hosted in a jail setting with popular streamers and internet stars role-playing as inmates and prison guards. The stream would feature artists and content creators such as Druski, Offset, YourRAGE, and NLE Choppa for its entire duration.

It did not take long for something controversial to happen in such a stream. The stream started on October 27th and would last for almost a week. On the first day itself, the stream has already been making waves on the Internet. The situation takes place between rapper NLE Choppa, who plays an inmate, and adult star Teanna Trump, who plays a prison guard.

In the stream, Kai Cenat and his friends will be in jail for seven days. They will be locked behind bars and on-camera 24/7. This particular situation took place during the daytime when most inmates could be seen hanging out in a common area. The CCTV footage then cuts to the public toilet, where NLE Choppa and Teanna Trump can be seen.
