Career

Xolo Mariduena debuted his career in the industry professionally by working in commercials and print ads at the age of 9 only. He grabbed a chance to play a prominent role in the award-winning American comedy-drama series “Parenthood” in 2012. He has landed cameo roles in the television shows titled “Mack & Moxy” and “Rush Hour”.

In 2013, he was cast as “Manny” in the comedy film “Dealin’ with Idiots”. He was also featured in a TV movie named “Furst Born”. He was a part of a game show “Noches con Platanito” in 2018. Apart from these, he has also made voice roles in the science fiction animated series “Cleopatra in Space” and in supernatural mystery animated series “Victor and Valentino”. His other significant acting projects include “Cobra Kai”, “Major Crimes”, “Twin Peaks”, The Netflix Afterparty” and others.
