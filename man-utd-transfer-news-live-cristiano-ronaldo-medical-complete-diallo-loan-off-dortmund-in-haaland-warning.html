Man Utd transfer news LIVE: Cristiano Ronaldo medical COMPLETE, Diallo loan OFF, Dortmund in Haaland

GOOD MORNING MAN UTD FANS

It’s not bad been a Red Devil at the moment.

28 games unbeaten on the road, another three points yesterday and a certain Cristiano Ronaldo back in red.

Not bad for a Bank Holiday.

There’s still plenty of transfer activity around Old Trafford though and loose ends to tie up before tomorrow’s deadline.

We’ve the very latest on Amad Diallo’s potential move out on loan, with an injury potentially scuppering his departure.

Ole has also revealed his plans for CR7 now he is back at the club, with the superstar set to play as a centre-forward.

And in the ongoing saga of Erling Haaland, Dortmund have again reiterated their stance.

Sporting Director Michael Zorc told Kicker: “We are a football club, not a bank! Our position is very clear and did not change.

“I assume that we will now be confronted with wild rumours for another two days.

“Our position is clear, I don’t need to play the parrot all the time.”
