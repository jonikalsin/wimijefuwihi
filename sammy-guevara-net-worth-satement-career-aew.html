Career Overview Of Sammy Guevara

Sammy Guevara is a professional wrestler born on 28 July 1993 in Houston, Texas.

Sammy Guevara, 28 age, started his training period in BookerT's Reality of Wrestling in his hometown Houston, Texas. He was passionate about professional wrestling from an early age after watching WWE Hall of Famer Shawn Michaels' performance via television.

He made his first appearance for a major professional wrestling promotion National Wrestling Alliance in 2013. Sammy partnered with Tony Guevara against Los Matadores named Diego & Fernando in the tag team dark match. Unfortunately, they lose on the 7 April 2015 edition of WWE Main Event.

Then, on 27 May 2016, Sammy had a victory over the former WWE NXT wrestler Jody Kristofferson in his debut match in a full impact wrestling pro match.

He debuted during the July 3rd edition of Xplosion, partnering with Davey Richards in a tag match which they lost to Drago & Taiji Ishimori.

During the July 4th edition of Impact wrestling, Guevara participated in the 2017 Super X Cup Tournament, where Drago eliminated him in the first round.

Additionally, the 28-years-old wrestler also played against the PWG World Champion Walter in the main event for the title but was unable to win against him. He faced lots of ups and downs during his early career, but he never gave up and worked hard to achieve his goal.

Then in 2017, he signed with Wrestling Guerrilla, and on 7 December 2017, Guevara made his first debut career for Major League Wrestling (MLW). He also made multiple appearances for MLW's television program MLW fusion in 2018.

In 2018 he signed Lucha Libre AAA Worldwide and his debut at Verano De Escandalo 2018. Sammy has won his first AAA World cruiserweight championship at the Triplemania XXVI annual event in 2018.

Happily, his first championship, titled PWFP Ultimate Championship, came into his bag on 28 April 2019, where he won against the then champion Raja Naveed.

The American wrestler Sammy has also faced Laredo Kid in 2019 but was unable to defeat him.

The 1.7 m tall wrestler was announced to signed with the newly formed All Elite Wrestling as a roaster on 7 February 2019. Cody Rhodes defeated him in the opening match of the inaugural episode of AEW Dynamite.

All Elite Wrestling star Sammy Guevara is popularly known as The Spanish God on AEW television, who lost his first major PPV match against Darby Allin at the AEW Revolution 2020.
